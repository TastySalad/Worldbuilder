# Dust_Storms

## Dust Storms

**Phenomenon Description:**
[[Dust_Storms]] are an omnipresent and defining hazard across much of [[The Sun-Scorched Lands]], particularly within [[The_Glimmering_Wastes]]. These are not mere gusts of wind, but colossal, rolling walls of sand and pulverized rock, often reaching heights of several kilometers and stretching for hundreds of kilometers across the landscape. They form rapidly, often heralded by a sudden drop in temperature and an eerie stillness, before a distant, reddish-brown wall appears on the horizon, swiftly engulfing everything in its path.

A particularly dangerous variant, common in [[The_Glimmering_Wastes]], are **[[Glimmer-Storms]]**. These are intensified [[Dust_Storms]] infused with the fine, silicate-rich sand that gives the wastes their name. The abrasive properties of this 'Glimmerdust' are magnified, capable of stripping paint from metal, scouring flesh from bone, and blinding unprepared individuals. Furthermore, [[Glimmer-Storms]] often carry strange, localized electrical charges, causing the air to crackle and glow with an eerie, intermittent light, and can disrupt ancient technology. The dust particles can penetrate even the most sealed structures, leaving a fine, shimmering layer over everything.

**Atmosphere:**
The approach of a [[Dust Storm]] plunges the world into a chilling silence before the roar begins. Once it hits, the atmosphere becomes a suffocating, blinding maelstrom. Visibility drops to mere meters, sometimes even centimeters, as the air fills with abrasive particles. The sky turns a uniform, oppressive brown or a sickly, glowing orange during a [[Glimmer-Storm]]. The sound is deafening: a constant, howling gale punctuated by the impact of larger debris, the groan of straining structures, and the desperate cries of those caught outside. Breathing becomes difficult, the dust clogging lungs and stinging eyes. Static electricity can build up, causing sparks to jump between objects, and during a [[Glimmer-Storm]], the air itself can hum with an unsettling energy. Navigating during a [[Dust Storm]] is virtually impossible, and any exposed skin is quickly flayed by the relentless abrasion. The experience is one of utter helplessness and profound isolation, a grim reminder of the world's indifference to life.
