# Glimmerdust

### Lore: Glimmerdust
[[Glimmerdust]] is a mystical, fine, iridescent powder found predominantly within [[The_Glimmering_Wastes]]. It is a crystalline mineral shimmering with an ethereal pale blue light. It is believed to be the residual essence of an ancient, cataclysmic magical event that transformed the region, or perhaps the crystallized magic of a fallen celestial body. Its most striking property is its faint, ethereal luminescence, especially potent in darkness. While Glimmerdust itself shimmers, the widespread "glimmering" effect of [[The_Glimmering_Wastes]] as a whole is primarily due to the vast [[Salt Flats]] and salt recrystallization. Beyond its beauty, [[Glimmerdust]] possesses powerful magical properties. As a potent psychoactive and an energy-rich mineral, it is the most coveted and contested resource in the region. In small doses, it can enhance magical spells, imbue objects with a temporary glow, or even act as a mild stimulant. However, prolonged exposure or ingestion in larger quantities can be dangerous, leading to vivid hallucinations, disorientation, and in extreme cases, permanent magical mutation or madness. Cultures living near [[The_Glimmering_Wastes]] have developed various methods for harvesting and refining [[Glimmerdust]], using it in everything from alchemical concoctions and potent medicines to dangerous poisons and powerful arcane rituals. Its volatile nature makes it a highly sought-after, yet feared, commodity.
