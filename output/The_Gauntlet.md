# The_Gauntlet

ENTITY: The Gauntlet (region)
PARENT: Barren Peaks
DESCRIPTION: A treacherous, deep chasm cutting through the heart of the [[Barren_Peaks]]. This formidable, glacially carved rift is characterized by towering, near-vertical granite and basalt cliffs rising hundreds of meters. Its floor is a chaotic jumble of colossal boulders, scree fields, and narrow, winding passages often choked with ice and snow; evidence of ancient glacial activity, such as polished rock faces, U-shaped valley sections, and moraines, is abundant throughout. The climate is an exceptionally harsh alpine tundra, with year-round sub-zero temperatures, brutal blizzards, and extensive permafrost, limiting direct sunlight and maintaining persistent ice formations. Powerful, channeled winds, including constant howling gales and katabatic blasts, dominate its atmosphere, scouring the rock faces and creating treacherous drifts.
